Sea temperature: North Tyneside
Satellite SST is derived from Copernicus Marine OSTIA analysis. The smartwatch series uses daily minimum readings and may reflect swim timing, depth, sensor behaviour and local nearshore conditions. Manual observations are transcribed from swim club logbooks and represent thermometer readings taken in the water. Original scans remain the source of truth. Interpolated satellite values shape the line; dots show actual satellite readings.
