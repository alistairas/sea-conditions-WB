Sea temperature is derived from the Copernicus Marine OSTIA analysis, produced by the UK Met Office using satellite and in-situ observations. Values represent the nearest available grid cell for the North Tyneside coast and should be treated as one source of information on local sea conditions, not a safety assessment.

Built as a reusable prototype for live sea temperature, local observations, and future comparison with historic PSC bathing water records.
